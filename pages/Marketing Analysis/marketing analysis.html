Ranging
Select the Preferred Regions
Select your preferred group of regions. Choose all of Russia, by federal district, or a special region list and rank them by the indicators you are interested in. Regions are grouped by development level as top, medium, and moderate. Work with the ones you find most suitable for your needs.

Compared Analysis
Define the Potential Resources
Compare indicators of your specified region(s) with other regions, with the Russian indicators, with the federal district indicators, with indicators of regions with top, medium, and moderate development level. Calculate the balance between indicators of your region of interest and other regions and define the potential resources to improve the development strategy of that region.

Statistical Modeling
Identify Each Region’s Trend Development
Establish statistical dependencies in the formation of economic potential and quality of life in the regions based on our data. Choose the optimal conditions for the development of your business.
  • Build Statistical Models and Install:
  • The relationship between indicators;
  • The impact of indicators on the resulting factor;
  • Simulation models of the development of the region and/or industry.
  • Build a Factorial Model and Determine:
  • The main factors in the development of the region;
  • Regional differences in the formation of the main factors;
  • A set of main factors in statistical modeling.
  • Build Forecast Statistical Models and Install:
  • Tends in the development of regions;
  • Scenarios for the development of regions.
  • Statistical Models
  • Build statistical model of the development region.
  • Select the main indicators of the region’s development from our Indicator Catalog.
  • Choose the resulting indicator (Y) and the indicators that form it (x1, x2, x3, ...).
  • Build a primary multifactor model Y = f (x1, x2, x3, ...) of the region’s development. Establish correlations.
  • Select the main factors and build a working simulation model for the region’s development.
