Region Profile
We provide comprehensive and up-to-data business profiles from the Russian market, federal district markets, and the 60 leading regions. Analyze the regions and estimate the possibilities of expanding your business in these regions.
Region Profile Data
General Reference:
Read a brief reference about the history, geography, nature, resources, region demographic, administrative structure, and regional government (contacts).
Economical Profile:
Create a regional economical profile by GRP, industrial and agricultural income, trade turnover, per capita income, salary, export, import, and foreign trade turnover size, and structure.
Ratings:
Analyze regional investment attractiveness and quality of life by ratings from international and national rating agencies.
Business Environmen:
Estimate each region's business environment based on the regional tax benefits and preferences, availability of the industrial parks, special economic zones, etc.
Business Structure:
Analyze regional business by company size, number of employees, and statistical ratings.
Competitive Environment:
Analyze company databases in a select region, in other regions, or in all of Russia. Study their production capabilities, economic potential, benefits, and problems.
Right Decision
Select the needed region and use our data to form your own business profile. Reach out to the regional government, analyze conditions of business development, contact companies working in the region, and study the competitive environment. Estimate your benefits and problems to decide if you want to open or expand your business in the region.
